01

What We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our money transfer service and comply with our legal obligations. This includes:

02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your money transfers;
  • Verify your identity and comply with anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC) regulations;
  • Detect and prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activity;
  • Send you transaction confirmations and service notifications;
  • Respond to your customer service requests;
  • Improve our app and services through anonymized usage analytics;
  • Comply with applicable law and regulatory requirements.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your transaction data to train third-party AI models. We do not send marketing emails unless you have explicitly opted in.

03

Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with:

  • Banking partners: To process and settle your transfers;
  • Identity verification providers: To fulfill our KYC obligations;
  • Payment processors: To charge your funding source securely;
  • Regulatory authorities: Where required by applicable law, including OFAC screening and FinCEN reporting;
  • Service providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and customer support tools — all under contractual data processing agreements.

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for the purposes we specify and to maintain appropriate security standards.

04

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Transaction records: 7 years (required by US federal and state money transmission laws);
  • Identity verification records: 5 years after account closure (AML requirements);
  • Account data: Duration of account plus 3 years;
  • Support communications: 2 years.

After applicable retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

05

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest;
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit;
  • Multi-factor authentication for all internal access to production systems;
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing;
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact support@akoma.app immediately.

06

Your Rights — GDPR (EU & UK)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

Right of access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to erasure

Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to restriction

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

Right to portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@akoma.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our legal bases for processing are: contract performance (transfers), legal obligation (AML/KYC), and legitimate interests (fraud prevention, service improvement).

07

Your Rights — CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used;
  • Delete personal information we hold about you (subject to exceptions);
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — Akoma does not sell personal information;
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@akoma.app with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity before processing your request.

08

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication and security. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymized usage data to improve our service (opt-out available).

We do not use advertising, retargeting, or third-party tracking cookies. Our mobile app does not use device advertising identifiers for marketing.

09

Children's Privacy

Akoma is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at privacy@akoma.app and we will delete it.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Akoma after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

The most current version of this policy is always available at akoma.app/privacy.
